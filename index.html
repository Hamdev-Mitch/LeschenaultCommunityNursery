About us

A Not-For Profit Organisation, which has been operating since 1998 in Bunbury Western Australia. LCN is managed by a committee board and employ 4 staff members who co-ordinate and assist volunteers in the daily running’s of the Nursery. At LCN we are dedicated to encouraging the use of local native plants in the revegetation and rehabilitation of lands, waterways, wetlands and coastal areas in the South West of Western Australia. Local plant communities provide the most suitable food and habitat for our local native wildlife and are most suited to grow in local conditions and will be more likely to thrive than plants that are not. Our nursery is open to the public for retail sales as well as wholesale, with over 200 varieties of Local native plants to choose from, suppling to landscapers, local government departments, rural landholders, schools and rehabilitation groups. LCN is an accredited member of the Nursery & Garden Industry of Western Australia and is a Water Corporation, Waterwise Nursery.
